about.

Hi, I'm Dalal, a data scientist building innovative data-driven solutions for cultural institutions. My work lies at the intersection of artificial intelligence, digital humanities, and the preservation of cultural heritage. I bring extensive experience in independently leading research projects and developing sophisticated data analysis tools, aiming to enhance the digital transformation of the cultural sector. Prior to being a data scientist, I worked as an observational astrophysicist developing data analysis code for large, multi-wavelength astronomical data taken with both space and ground-based telescopes.
